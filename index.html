Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simmply Pairs, is a card game in which all the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

About the game

This game will take every ounce of concentration and memorisation to win. Good luck!
